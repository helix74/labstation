## Phase 1: Discovery (The "Signal")

- **Touchpoint**: Instagram Story / Word of Mouth.
- **Action**: User clicks link `labstation.tn`.
- **Expectation**: "Let's see what Moudi has cooked up."

## Phase 2: Entry (The "Boot Sequence")

- **Touchpoint**: Website Loader.
- **Action**: Waits for load.
- **Experience**: "Accessing Lab..." animation. The Scientist character appears.
- **Feeling**: Intrigue. Excitement. "This isn't a normal site."

## Phase 3: Immersion (The "Lab Tour")

- **Touchpoint**: Hero Section.
- **Action**: Interacts with the "Launch Protocol" button.
- **Experience**: The page reveals the "Lab". Dark, industrial, glowing. The Scientist welcomes them.
- **Feeling**: Immersed. "I'm in a video game."

## Phase 4: Exploration (The "Experiments")

- **Touchpoint**: Menu Section.
- **Action**: Hovers over "Ribs Burger".
- **Experience**: The card expands. Smoke effect. Ingredients listed as "Chemical Components".
- **Feeling**: Hunger + Amusement. "Look at this detail!"

## Phase 5: Validation (The "Proof")

- **Touchpoint**: Testimonials Section.
- **Action**: Reads "Best Burger in Ariana" reviews.
- **Experience**: Sees real +1000 reviews scrolling.
- **Feeling**: Trust. "Okay, it's legit."

## Phase 6: Action (The "Mission")

- **Touchpoint**: Location / Footer.
- **Action**: Clicks "Get Directions" or calls.
- **Result**: Visits the restaurant.
